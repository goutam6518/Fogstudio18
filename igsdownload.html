How to Download
Click on download button and open APKadmin download page. Wait 5 seconds and click on "Download file". An ad may open—click back and then click on "Download file" again.

Click on "Start Download". After loading, the download will start automatically. If it doesn't, click the 3-dot menu and then tap "Download".

Once the file is downloaded, tap it. You’ll see the install screen. Tap "Install".

Your app will appear on your home screen. Enjoy using IGS!
